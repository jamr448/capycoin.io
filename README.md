# 🪙 Capycoin

Capycoin is a community-driven memecoin inspired by the calm and friendly spirit of the capybara.

Built for the WorldChain ecosystem, Capycoin focuses on community growth, viral distribution, and fun participation through airdrops and social engagement.

---

# 🚀 Capycoin Airdrop

Early supporters can register their WorldChain wallet to participate in the Capycoin airdrop.

Steps to participate:

1. Visit the official Capycoin website
2. Enter your WorldChain wallet address
3. Join the Telegram community
4. Wait for the official airdrop distribution
